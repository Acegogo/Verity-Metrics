## Selected Approach: Scandinavian Corporate Minimalism meets African Warmth

I'm going with the first approach because it best serves Verity Metrics' brand identity:
- The light blue color scheme is prominent and professional
- Magazine-style layouts showcase the AI-generated photography beautifully
- Clean whitespace communicates trust and competence
- Warm accents honor the African context of the work
- The design feels modern and sleek without being cold
